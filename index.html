About Me

My Ph.D. specialization is in Artificial Intelligence and Data Mining. I have an extensive background in machine learning, data science, computer visualization, and human behavior. In the Ph.D. program, I work under the mentorship of faculty advisors Dr. Mukhopadhyay and Dr. Fang in the Computer and Information Science Department at the Purdue School of Science, Indianapolis. Through my research, I aim to advance AI in education using novel machine learning techniques that combine data analysis and signal processing. Additional areas are in adversarial robustness related to human behavior and ethics.
